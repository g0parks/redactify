About

redactify/about.md

Redactify is a private practice. Bespoke engagements. NDA by default. You talk to the person who'd run the work.

I've worked both sides. Black hat curiosity, white hat engagement. The first taught me how things break. The second taught me how to break them on purpose, under contract, with a report at the end.

The work ranges. Adversarial testing and red team operations. Threat assessments for people who've become a target. Organizational research before a sensitive decision. Response work when something private has gone public. One recent client came to us after their private images were leaked. Another was a principal who wanted to know who was watching them before they made a move. None of it lives on a service menu.

AI agents do most of the research. They run on secure local hardware, never exposed publicly. No public model calls, no cloud pipelines. Client data stays on systems we control.

We're not a firm, we're a network. Investigators, operators, privacy engineers, attorneys on call. If the job needs a hand we don't have in-house, we know who does. The threat landscape keeps evolving. We stay ahead of it.
